Crystal Collector
Instructions:

There are four Crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Note: each time the game starts, the game will change the values of the crystal. Goodluck!!
